Background

Myself

My name is Puseletso Mothobi, a young Mosotho woman from Ha Foso,Berea. I grew up in a large chiristan family, surrounded by my cousins, uncles and aunties. From a young age, I developed a deep love for reading and writing, which eventually led me to explore creative writing. I enjoy crafting stories and essays that allow me to express my thougts and emotions and have a passion for books. I have always been intrigued by organizing knowledge and focusing on details, this interest has led me to pursue librarianship. I attended Unity English Medium School for my primary education and attended Methodist High School for my LGCSE. I am an aspiring information specialist currently enrolled at the National University of Lesotho, studying Library and Information Studies and I am in my forth year. My skills include problem solving, teamwork, interpersonal communication, critical thining, a strong work ethic, emotional intelligence and web development just to mention a few.
